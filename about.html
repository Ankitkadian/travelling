About

The Founder:

Fahad Saleemis a UK-based Pakistani, he went to the UK 11 years ago to pursue his undergraduate and mastersdegrees. Upon completing his degrees, he began working in the Higher Education industry. Mainly managingthe international educational partnerships, student recruitment and business development, Fahad saw for himself the limited prevalence of overseas products in Pakistan and the reliance placed on young students’ baggage allowance from long-lost cousins and a neighbour’s sister’s watchman’s roommate. Fahad came up with the idea for LayAoo in response to this.

We are here to bring authentic, cost-effective products to your doorsteps. LayAoo is founded with the aim to provide every Pakistani with a choice: that is the opportunity of buying organic, authentic, and cost-effective products from all around the world with minimum fuss.

LayAoo promises to connect you with our listed travellers from all around the world to get imported products to your doorstep.

Why choose us?

  1. We are first of our kind, bringing this concept to a Pakistani audience.
  2. Ease of use
  3. Large community of travellers and shoppers in one place
  4. We ensure 100% safety of your funds, not releasing till you are satisfied with your purchase. Our travellers endeavor to supply you with receipts and you can carry out inspections before approving your purchase
  5. Traveller incentives
  6. Pick and drop service centre in major cities of Pakistan and further plans of expansion to cover cities nationwide.

Our Core values

COST:

Products requested through our platform are extremely cost effective compared to other solutions. On average, we are 40% cheaper than all our competition. You ask HOW? Well, YOU choose the price and we cut off all the middlemen. Our pricing is very, very simple. There are no complicated algorithms. We charge 7% on the purchase price and a fee of £2 to cover banking and service charges. The traveller bringing in your product is just another helpful user of our platform who makes some money for his service.

Giving traveller and requester (shoppers) the freedom to agree on traveller remuneration is something we are very proud of.

CONVENIENCE:

  1. We take care of all security and safety measures.
  2. Shoppers and traveller don’t have to meet to exchange products. You can simply pick and drop off the products at one of your regional collection/pick up points OR choose a delivery option (at an additional- yet nominal - cost).
  3. To safeguard our traveller and shoppers’ privacy, we allow you to either display your photo on the profile, or not.

CHOICE:

We are here to spoil you with choices! With travellers coming from all over the world at your fingertips, you can literally get anything from anywhere - as long as it’s legal.

COMFORT:

With just a few click on our website, you can connect with hundreds of members who are ordering and travelling every week.

COMPETITION:

We are cheaper and faster than all our competitors. We are simply the best because no such marketplace like this has ever existed in Pakistan and we truly want to get you what you want, when you want it and at the price you’re comfortable paying..

OUR VISION:

At LayAoo, our vision is to break down barriers, allowing everyone access to enjoy their favorite products, no matter what country they come from
