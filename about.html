About

The Founder:

Fahad Saleemis a UK-based Pakistani, he went to the UK 11 years ago to pursue his undergraduate and mastersdegrees. Upon completing his degrees, he began working in the Higher Education industry. Mainly managingthe international educational partnerships, student recruitment and business development, Fahad saw for himself the limited prevalence of overseas products in Pakistan and the reliance placed on young students’ baggage allowance from long-lost cousins and a neighbour’s sister’s watchman’s roommate. Fahad came up with the idea for LayAoo in response to this.

We are here to bring authentic, cost-effective products to your doorsteps. LayAoo is founded with the aim to provide every Pakistani with a choice: that is the opportunity of buying organic, authentic, and cost-effective products from all around the world with minimum fuss.

LayAoo promises to connect you with our listed travellers from all around the world to get imported products to your doorstep.
